Chihuahua

About the Chihuahua

The Chihuahua is the smallest dog breed, known for its big personality in a tiny package. They are highly devoted to their owners and can be very protective despite their small size. They are lively, alert, and full of energy.

Characteristics

Chihuahuas have a distinctive apple-shaped head, large eyes, and erect ears. They are often seen as lap dogs but require proper training and socialization to ensure they don't become overly possessive or aggressive.

Care

Chihuahuas need minimal grooming but should be brushed regularly to keep their coat in good condition. They are prone to dental issues, so regular dental care is essential. They enjoy short walks and playtime but should not be overexerted.
