Labrador Retriever

About the Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States. Friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions, Labradors have a wide range of appeal for all sorts of people and families.

Characteristics

Labradors are known for their kind eyes, loyalty, and playfulness. They make great family pets and are excellent with children. They are also known for their intelligence, which makes them easy to train.

Care

Labradors require regular exercise to stay healthy and happy. They love activities like fetching, swimming, and hiking. Regular grooming is also important to keep their coat in good condition.
