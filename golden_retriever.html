Golden Retriever

About the Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a highly popular breed known for its friendly and tolerant attitude. They are intelligent, eager to please, and great with families and children.

Characteristics

Golden Retrievers are known for their beautiful golden coats, friendly nature, and high energy levels. They are excellent family dogs and are often used as therapy dogs due to their gentle temperament.

Care

Golden Retrievers require regular exercise and grooming to keep their coats in good condition. They love outdoor activities and need regular brushing to manage shedding and maintain their coat health.
