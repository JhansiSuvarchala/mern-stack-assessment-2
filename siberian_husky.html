Siberian Husky

About the Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working dog known for its wolf-like appearance and endurance. They are friendly, alert, and outgoing, making them excellent family pets for active households. Their intelligence and independent nature can sometimes make them a challenge to train.

Characteristics

Siberian Huskies have a thick double coat that comes in a variety of colors and markings. They have striking blue or multicolored eyes and erect triangular ears. They are known for their boundless energy and love for running.

Care

Siberian Huskies require regular exercise to prevent boredom and maintain their health. Their thick coat needs to be brushed regularly, especially during shedding seasons. They are generally healthy dogs but require regular veterinary check-ups to ensure their well-being.
