Boxer

About the Boxer

Boxers are energetic, fun-loving dogs known for their boundless energy and affection for their families. They have a strong, muscular build and are highly intelligent, making them excellent working dogs and loyal companions.

Characteristics

Boxers have a distinct, strong appearance with a square jaw and a short muzzle. They are highly playful and require lots of exercise. Their natural protective instincts make them great watchdogs, though they are generally friendly and social.

Care

Boxers need plenty of physical activity and mental stimulation to prevent boredom. Regular exercise and a balanced diet are essential for keeping them healthy. Their short coat requires minimal grooming, but they do shed moderately.
