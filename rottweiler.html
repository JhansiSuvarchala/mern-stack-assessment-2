Rottweiler

About the Rottweiler

Rottweilers are confident, fearless dogs with a robust build. They are known for their loyalty and protective nature, making them excellent guard dogs. Originally bred to drive cattle to market, they are strong, hardworking, and versatile dogs.

Characteristics

Rottweilers have a powerful, muscular body with a broad chest and a solid frame. They are known for their calm demeanor but can be very protective of their families. They require a firm, consistent hand in training.

Care

Rottweilers need regular exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and healthy. They require a balanced diet to maintain their muscular physique. Grooming needs are moderate, with regular brushing to keep their coat in good condition.
