Poodle

About the Poodle

Poodles are highly intelligent and elegant dogs, known for their hypoallergenic curly coats. They come in three sizes: Standard, Miniature, and Toy, all of which are known for their lively and playful nature.

Characteristics

Poodles are active and love to be the center of attention. They are known for their versatility, excelling in obedience, agility, and even as therapy dogs. Their curly coats require regular grooming to keep them looking their best.

Care

Poodles need regular exercise to stay mentally and physically stimulated. They require consistent grooming, including regular baths, haircuts, and ear cleanings to prevent infections.
