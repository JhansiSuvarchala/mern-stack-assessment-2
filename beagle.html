Beagle

About the Beagle

The Beagle is a small to medium-sized breed known for its keen sense of smell and strong hunting instincts. They are friendly, curious, and great with children.

Characteristics

Beagles have a distinctive appearance with their floppy ears and expressive eyes. They are energetic, playful, and very loyal to their families.

Care

Beagles require regular exercise to stay healthy and prevent obesity. They have a short coat that is easy to groom, but they do shed regularly.
