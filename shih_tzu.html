Shih Tzu

About the Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is a toy dog breed known for its long, flowing coat and affectionate nature. They are friendly, outgoing, and thrive on human companionship. They make excellent companions for families, including those with children.

Characteristics

Shih Tzus have a distinctive look with their round face, short snout, and large, dark eyes. They are known for their long, luxurious coat, which requires regular grooming. Despite their small size, they have a confident and sturdy build.

Care

Shih Tzus require regular grooming to maintain their coat, including daily brushing to prevent tangles and mats. They are relatively low-energy dogs but enjoy short walks and playtime. Regular veterinary care is important to monitor their overall health, especially their dental health.
